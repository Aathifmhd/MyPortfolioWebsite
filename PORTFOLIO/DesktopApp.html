Wildlife HMS

WildLife HMS is a collaborative group project aimed at developing an advanced Habitat Management System. This innovative desktop application leverages cutting-edge technology to assist conservationists, researchers, and environmentalists in the preservation and monitoring of ecosystems. By streamlining data collection, analysis, and decision-making processes, the project showcases teamwork, technical expertise, and a commitment to environmental sustainability. Designed as part of our academic journey at the University of Ruhuna, this project demonstrates our ability to solve complex problems and contribute to impactful solutions.
